Matching Siding on South Orange's Historic Homes

South Orange's Victorian and historic homes often feature unique siding profiles and materials that can be challenging to match. Our extensive experience and supplier relationships allow us to source matching materials for even the oldest homes. We match wood grain patterns, profile dimensions, colors, and textures so repairs blend seamlessly with your existing siding.

Preventing Future Siding Damage

South Orange's mature tree canopy creates unique challenges for home exteriors. Falling branches, accumulated moisture, and insect activity can damage siding. Beyond repairs, we provide recommendations for protecting your siding investment, including regular maintenance schedules, protective treatments for wood siding, proper gutter systems, and landscaping adjustments to prevent moisture problems.
