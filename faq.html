Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common roofing questions

How often should I have my roof inspected?

We recommend having your roof professionally inspected at least once a year, preferably in the spring or fall. Additionally, you should schedule an inspection after any major storm event, especially if you notice visible damage or leaks.

What are the signs I need a new roof?

Common signs include: missing or damaged shingles, water stains on ceilings, daylight through roof boards, sagging roof deck, excessive granules in gutters, and a roof that's over 20 years old. If you notice any of these signs, contact us for a free inspection.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements can be completed in 1-3 days, depending on the size and complexity of your roof. Weather conditions and the type of roofing material chosen can also affect the timeline. We'll provide you with a detailed schedule before starting work.

Will my homeowner's insurance cover roof replacement?

Insurance coverage depends on the cause of damage and your specific policy. Storm damage, hail, and wind damage are typically covered. We work directly with insurance companies and can help you navigate the claims process to ensure you receive the coverage you're entitled to.

What roofing materials do you recommend?

We offer a variety of high-quality materials including asphalt shingles, metal roofing, tile, and slate. The best choice depends on your budget, aesthetic preferences, and local climate. Our experts will help you choose the perfect material for your home.

What warranty do you provide?

We offer comprehensive warranties on both materials and workmanship. Most roofing materials come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 20-50 years. Additionally, we provide our own workmanship warranty to ensure your complete satisfaction and peace of mind.

Do you offer emergency roof repair services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency roofing services. If you have a roof leak or storm damage that needs immediate attention, call us anytime. We'll dispatch our emergency team to protect your property and prevent further damage.
