Frequently Asked Questions About Gutter Repair & Cleaning

Common questions from Irvington homeowners

Most homes need gutter cleaning twice a year - in late spring and late fall. Homes with many trees may need cleaning 3-4 times annually. Regular cleaning prevents clogs and water damage.

Clogged gutters cause water to overflow, damaging your foundation, basement, siding, and landscaping. In winter, they can create ice dams that damage your roof. Regular cleaning prevents these expensive problems.

Yes! We repair gutter leaks at seams, joints, and holes. We use professional-grade sealants and can replace damaged sections if needed. Most leak repairs are completed the same day.

Sagging occurs when hangers are spaced too far apart, when gutters are clogged with heavy debris, or when hangers deteriorate. We reinforce with new hangers spaced properly to prevent future sagging.
