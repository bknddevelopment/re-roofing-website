Complete Roof Replacement for Cedar Grove Homes

Many Cedar Grove homes feature original roofs from the 1960s-1980s construction boom, and these aging systems are now reaching the end of their service life. Combined with the stress from Cedar Grove's mature tree canopy—which drops debris, promotes moss growth, and creates shade that holds moisture—these roofs require complete replacement to ensure continued protection for your home.

R&E Roofing specializes in roof replacement for the ranch and split-level architectural styles common throughout Cedar Grove. We understand the unique structural considerations of these homes, from proper ventilation requirements to ensuring adequate drainage on lower-pitch roofs. Our complete replacement process includes thorough deck inspection, updated ventilation systems, and premium roofing materials designed to last 30-50 years.

Signs Your Cedar Grove Home Needs Roof Replacement:

Cedar Grove homeowners should consider complete roof replacement if their roof is over 20 years old, shows widespread shingle curling or loss of granules, has multiple areas of moss or algae growth, or has experienced repeated leaks despite repairs. The combination of age and environmental stress from overhanging trees often means that partial repairs are no longer cost-effective compared to complete replacement.

Additionally, many original roofs on Cedar Grove's ranch and split-level homes lack adequate ventilation, leading to premature aging from heat and moisture buildup in the attic. A complete roof replacement allows us to upgrade your ventilation system, improving energy efficiency and extending the life of your new roof while preventing future ice dam problems during New Jersey winters.
