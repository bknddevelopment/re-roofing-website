Expert Roofing for Cedar Grove's Established Neighborhoods

Cedar Grove is a beautiful residential township known for its tree-lined streets, well-maintained homes, and strong sense of community. With a population of approximately 12,980 residents, Cedar Grove features predominantly ranch-style and split-level homes built between the 1950s and 1980s, creating unique roofing challenges that require specialized expertise.

R&E Roofing has been serving Cedar Grove homeowners for over 25 years, providing expert roofing, siding, and gutter services throughout the township. We understand the specific needs of Cedar Grove homes, from the mature tree canopy that requires diligent gutter maintenance to the architectural styles that demand careful material selection and installation techniques.

Cedar Grove Neighborhoods We Serve:

  • Lindsley Park Area - Beautiful wooded neighborhood with mature homes
  • North Cedar Grove - Established residential area with split-level homes
  • Memorial Drive - Tree-lined streets with ranch-style homes
  • Bradford Avenue - Quiet residential streets near town center
  • Pompton Avenue Corridor - Accessible location with diverse housing
  • South Cedar Grove - Family-friendly neighborhoods with excellent schools

Roofing Challenges Specific to Cedar Grove:

Cedar Grove's abundant tree coverage creates a beautiful natural environment, but it also presents specific roofing challenges. Overhanging branches can deposit leaves, twigs, and debris on roofs and in gutters, accelerating wear and increasing the risk of water damage. Many Cedar Grove homes feature the ranch and split-level architectural styles popular in the 1960s and 1970s, with lower-pitch roofs that require proper drainage and careful waterproofing.

We also understand that Cedar Grove homeowners take pride in their community's appearance. Our team works carefully to select roofing materials and colors that complement your home's architecture while meeting the township's standards. Whether you're dealing with storm damage from severe weather, aging shingles that need replacement, or simply want to upgrade to more energy-efficient roofing materials, R&E Roofing provides solutions tailored to Cedar Grove homes.

Our Cedar Grove Roofing Services Include:

  • Complete roof replacements for aging ranch and split-level homes
  • Emergency repairs for storm damage and leaks
  • Gutter installation with guards to handle leaf buildup from mature trees
  • Vinyl and steel siding installation and repair
  • Roof inspections and maintenance programs
  • Insurance claim assistance for storm damage
