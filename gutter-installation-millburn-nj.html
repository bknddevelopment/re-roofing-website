Frequently Asked Questions About Gutter Installation

Common questions from Millburn homeowners

Seamless gutters are custom-formed on-site to fit your home perfectly with no seams except at corners. This eliminates leak points, looks better, and lasts longer than sectional gutters.

Gutter guards are highly recommended, especially if you have trees nearby. They prevent clogs, reduce maintenance, prevent ice dams, and protect your gutters from damage. The investment pays off in reduced maintenance.

Most homes use 5-inch or 6-inch gutters. We assess your roof size, pitch, and rainfall patterns to recommend the right size. Larger roofs or steep pitches may need 6-inch gutters for adequate capacity.

Aluminum gutters come in over 20 colors including white, black, brown, bronze, and various grays. We help you select a color that complements your siding, trim, and overall home aesthetic.

Quality aluminum gutters last 20-30 years with proper maintenance. Copper gutters can last 50+ years. Regular cleaning and prompt repair of any damage ensures maximum lifespan.
