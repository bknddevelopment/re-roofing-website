Common Siding Problems in Orange Homes

Orange homeowners face several common siding challenges due to our climate, tree cover, and the age of many homes:

  • Storm Damage: High winds can loosen or crack vinyl siding panels
  • Wood Rot: Moisture damage on older Victorian and Colonial homes with wood siding
  • Fading & Discoloration: UV damage on south-facing walls
  • Warping: Heat-related damage, especially on darker colored siding
  • Impact Damage: Damage from falling branches, hail, or accidents
  • Loose Panels: Wind damage or improper installation causing panels to detach
  • Cracking: Age-related brittleness or impact damage
