Premium Roof Replacement for South Orange's Distinguished Homes

A new roof is one of the most important investments you can make in your South Orange home. Whether you're replacing an aging asphalt shingle roof, restoring a historic slate roof on a Victorian home, or upgrading to energy-efficient premium materials, R&E Roofing delivers exceptional craftsmanship that enhances your home's value, curb appeal, and protection for decades to come.

Our South Orange Roof Replacement Services Include:

  • Premium Architectural Shingles - GAF Timberline HDZ, CertainTeed Landmark series with lifetime warranties
  • Slate Roof Installation - Authentic Vermont and Pennsylvania slate for historic homes
  • Synthetic Slate & Shake - DaVinci Roofscapes for historic appearance with modern durability
  • Metal Roofing - Standing seam and architectural metal for contemporary and traditional styles
  • Complete Tear-Off & Disposal - Proper removal of old roofing materials and debris
  • Structural Repairs - Deck replacement and structural reinforcement as needed
  • Advanced Underlayment Systems - Synthetic underlayment and ice/water barriers
