Craftsmanship That Shows in Every Detail

The difference between average roofers and exceptional ones shows in the details. Our New Jersey roofers understand that quality roofing isn't just about laying shingles—it's about proper preparation, precise installation, and attention to every component that makes your roof system work:

Proper Ventilation: Good roofers know that ventilation is critical for roof longevity. Our team ensures proper intake and exhaust ventilation to prevent moisture buildup, ice dams, and premature material failure.

Flashing Excellence: Flashing around chimneys, vents, skylights, and valleys is where most leaks occur. Our roofers take extra care with flashing installation, using quality materials and proven techniques to create watertight seals.

Deck Preparation: Professional roofers check and repair the roof deck before installing new materials. We replace damaged decking, ensure proper spacing, and create a solid foundation for your new roof.

Ice and Water Shield: In areas prone to ice dams and wind-driven rain, our roofers install ice and water shield protection in valleys, eaves, and other vulnerable areas—going beyond minimum code requirements for maximum protection.

Questions to Ask Potential Roofers

When interviewing roofers NJ homeowners should ask:

  1. Are the roofers employees or subcontractors? Direct employees mean better quality control and accountability.
  2. What training have your roofers received? Look for manufacturer certifications and ongoing education.
  3. How long have your roofers been with your company? Low turnover indicates good working conditions and experienced teams.
  4. Do you conduct background checks? Professional companies screen all employees.
  5. What safety measures do you employ? Proper safety equipment and training are non-negotiable.
  6. Who supervises the work? Experienced crew leaders ensure quality throughout the project.
  7. How do you handle cleanup? Daily debris removal and magnetic sweeps for nails should be standard.
