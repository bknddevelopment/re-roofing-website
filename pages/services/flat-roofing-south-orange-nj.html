Flat Roofing FAQs for South Orange Homeowners

What type of flat roof is best for South Orange commercial buildings?

TPO and EPDM are most popular for South Orange commercial properties. TPO offers energy efficiency and durability, while EPDM provides proven performance at lower cost. We assess your building and budget to recommend the optimal system.

How do you prevent pooling water on flat roofs in South Orange?

Proper drainage is critical in South Orange's climate. We design tapered insulation systems and install scuppers, drains, and gutters to ensure water flows off your flat roof efficiently, preventing leaks and extending roof life.

Can I install solar panels on my South Orange flat roof?

Absolutely! Flat roofs in South Orange are ideal for solar panel installation. We coordinate with solar installers or can integrate mounting systems during roof installation, ensuring proper waterproofing around penetrations.

How long do flat roofs last in South Orange?

Flat roofing systems in South Orange typically last 15-30 years depending on material and maintenance. TPO and PVC systems often reach 20-30 years, while EPDM averages 15-25 years with proper care.

Do flat roofs require more maintenance in South Orange?

Flat roofs benefit from bi-annual inspections in South Orange to clear debris, check drains, and inspect seams. Regular maintenance prevents small issues from becoming major leaks and maximizes your roof's lifespan.
