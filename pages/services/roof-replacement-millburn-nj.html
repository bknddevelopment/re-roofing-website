Luxury Roof Replacement Solutions

Millburn and Short Hills estates deserve exceptional roof replacement that enhances property value and architectural distinction. We specialize in premium roofing systems including natural slate, clay tile, cedar shake, copper, and high-performance architectural shingles that provide beauty and protection for generations.

  • Architectural Shingles - GAF, CertainTeed Luxury collections
  • Copper Roofing - Standing seam and flat seam systems
  • Historic Restoration - Period-appropriate materials and techniques
