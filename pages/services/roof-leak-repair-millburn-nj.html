Frequently Asked Questions About Roof Leaks

Common questions from Millburn homeowners about leak repair

We use a combination of visual inspection, infrared thermal imaging, and moisture detection equipment. Leaks often travel along rafters or decking before appearing inside, so we trace water stains back to their source on the roof. Our advanced equipment can detect hidden moisture you can't see.

The most common causes include: damaged or missing shingles, deteriorated flashing around chimneys and vents, clogged gutters causing water backup, cracked chimney crowns, failed skylight seals, ice dams in winter, and aging roofing materials. Each requires a different repair approach.

Absolutely. Even a small leak can lead to rotted roof decking, damaged insulation, ceiling and wall stains, mold growth, and electrical hazards. Water damage compounds quickly, so it's critical to address leaks immediately—even minor ones—to prevent costly repairs later.

Costs vary based on the leak's location and cause. Simple shingle repairs might cost $300-$800, while flashing repairs range from $500-$1,500. Complex leaks involving structural damage can cost $1,500-$3,000+. We provide free leak detection and detailed estimates before starting work.

Yes! We back all leak repairs with a written warranty. Before completing the job, we perform water tests to ensure the leak is fully resolved. If a repaired area leaks again during the warranty period, we'll return to fix it at no additional charge.
