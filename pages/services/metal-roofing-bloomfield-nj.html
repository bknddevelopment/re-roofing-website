Metal Roofing FAQs for Bloomfield Homeowners

How long do metal roofs last in Bloomfield?

Metal roofs in Bloomfield typically last 40-70 years - often outlasting the building itself. With proper installation, metal roofing can withstand Essex County's harsh winters, summer heat, and severe storms for decades.

Are metal roofs noisy during rain in Bloomfield?

No. Modern metal roofs with proper insulation and underlayment are actually quieter than asphalt shingles during Bloomfield's rainstorms. Our installation includes sound-dampening materials for a peaceful interior.

Will a metal roof lower my energy bills in Bloomfield?

Yes! Metal roofs reflect solar heat, reducing cooling costs by 10-25% during Bloomfield's hot summers. Many Bloomfield homeowners see ROI through energy savings within 10-15 years.

What metal roofing styles are available for my Bloomfield home?

We offer standing seam, metal shingles, metal tiles, and corrugated panels in various colors and finishes. Our team helps Bloomfield homeowners choose styles that complement traditional or modern architecture.

Can you install metal roofing over my existing roof in Bloomfield?

In many Bloomfield homes, yes - metal roofing can be installed over one layer of asphalt shingles, saving tear-off costs. We assess your roof structure to determine the best installation method.
